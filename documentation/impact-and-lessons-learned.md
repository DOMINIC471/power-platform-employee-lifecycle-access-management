# Impact and Lessons Learned

## Impact

The solution reduced manual work by replacing Word document-based access requests and separate email follow-ups with a centralised Power Apps process.

Key improvements:

- HR only needs to submit one structured form.
- Approvals are routed automatically.
- IT receives a structured request notification.
- IT checklist items are generated automatically.
- Checklist progress is tracked centrally.
- Auditability is improved through SharePoint records.

## Estimated Process Improvement

The previous manual process could take around 1-2 weeks depending on email follow-up, approver availability, and manual IT interpretation.

With automation, the process can potentially be completed in around 1-3 days where approvals and IT actions are completed promptly.

## Lessons Learned

- Approval logic should be separated from checklist generation.
- Parent/child SharePoint lists make audit history easier to track.
- Deep links improve user experience by opening the correct request and section.
- Service account ownership is important for long-term maintainability.
- Checklist generation reduces the risk of missed IT setup/offboarding tasks.
